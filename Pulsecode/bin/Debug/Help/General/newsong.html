Creating a new song

First off, go into editor screen and click "New Song". A dialog should appear, prompting you for the mp3 and background image. Drag and drop the mp3 and background image individually onto the form, and fill out the form fields accordingly. After pressing create, a new song should appear, and the mp3 and bg should have been copied. If you don't see your new song, reload by pressing f1.
